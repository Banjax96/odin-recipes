Classic Macaroni Salad

Macaroni Salad

Elbow macaroni, mayo, and various veggies

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Condiments (mayo and mustard)
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables (celery, onion, bell pepper)

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni in salted water.
  2. Mix together mayonaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, and seasonings.
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients. Chill in the refrigerator.
