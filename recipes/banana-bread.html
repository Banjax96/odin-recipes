Banana Bread

Tasty bread that is mouth watering

Ingredients

  • AP Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas (overripe)

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat butter and sugar together in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in preheated oven.
